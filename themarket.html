Competitor Analysis

The Wandering Chef offers a unique alternative to conventional food truck businesses, providing customers with a premium ‘personal chef’ style catering service. Not restricted by any specific cuisine, the business is able to satisfy a wider range of culinary preferences and provide each catering event with a unique set of menu options, depending on the ingredients provided by the customers. Finally, the business operates on a ‘per hour’ basis unlike its competitors. Similar to an all-you-can-eat restaurant, this business model offers customers a sense of value, thus further putting The Wandering Chef above its competitors.
