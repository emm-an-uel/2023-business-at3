About

The Wandering Chef is a food truck business which addresses the common problems of existing food truck businesses, namely the limited choice of menu and the lack of the option for customers to participate in the food selection process. With a fully equipped kitchen and a generator for self-powering capabilities, The Wandering Chef offers customers the unique business model of providing our professional chefs with personally sourced ingredients. Our chefs then create meals based on the provided ingredients and the customers' cuisine preferences.

Besides the food truck hire, The Wandering Chef also offers customers a custom meal prep service. A monthly subscription entitles customers to a complete service, from purchasing requested groceries to delivering meals to their homes.

  • Fully equipped kitchen
  • Professional chefs
  • Customer-provided ingredients
  • Custom menu options
  • Generator-equipped

Value Proposition

The Wandering Chef offers a unique business model which provides customers a platform to explore new cuisines based on their self-sources ingredients.

A strong demand in the region for such a business was determined through survey data which revealed that 57.1% of respondents were interested in such a catering business. Moreover, the meal prep service provides customers with the opportunity to be involved in the creation of a custom meny for the week through providing the business with either a specific grocery list or meal ideas. Unlike other meal prep businesses with a limited list of pre-selected menu items, The Wandering Chef's meal prep service provides customers with the experience of having a personal chef, thus tackling their pains of having to spend time and effort preparing food.

Registration

Customers interested in the meal prep service can subscribe through an online payment platform, Square, which provides customers with a convenient and safe alternative to the conventional process of registering via phone calls. Square streamlines the booking process and reduces the manpower required for the business' operastion, thus saving costs. Moreover, Square enables customers to make bookings and register for subscriptions directly from social media apps, thus closing the sale more efficiently.

Business Operation

Structure and Ownership

Business debts, profits, and capital investment costs will be split evenly between co-owners.

Locations and Outlets

The Wandering Chef has a food truck with a fully equipped kitchen so all preparation can be done without making a mess in in the customers' homes. Additionally, the truck as a generator which can power it for up to 6 hours, allowing the business to operate even in locations without access to mains power. The fully mobile nature of the business enables it to have a large operational area with ballpark figures estimating an average of 50 monthly events.

The business also has a rental kitchen in Belair to keep stock for and fulfil its prep service orders. Since the business' primary service, the food truck hire, operastes around Belair, having a kitchen in the area allows for ease of delivery to similar customer demographics.

Legal Considerations

The Wandering Chef is required to notify the council where the truck is normally garaged and the areas in which the business operates. Additionally, it is required to follow the below regulations outlining standards such as directions for food use and storage, date marking of food, and other safety practices:

  • Food Act 2001
  • Food Regulations 2017
  • Food Standards Code
  • Public Health (Notifiable Contaminants) Regulations 2020
